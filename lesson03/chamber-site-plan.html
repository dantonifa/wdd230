San Pedro Sula Chamber of Commerce Site Plan.

This name represents a city in which the chamber operates.

Purpose of the site-purpose

The purpose of the site is to provide information about the business community in San Pedro Sula, Honduras; the opportunities to develop a business in Industry, Commerce, Services, and Agriculture.

scenarios

  1. A business owner wants to know the requirements to open a business in san Pedro Sula.

  2. What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?

  3. Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?

  4. What are the benefits of becoming a member of the chamber?

Color Scheme

  • The color scheme will be based on the colors of the San Pedro Sula flag.

  • The header and footer background will be 7cb518

  • The Navigation bar and the main content background will be ffea00.

Typography

  • The site will use the Arial font family.

  • The font size for the main content will be 16px.
